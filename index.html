About Our Team

What is FTC?

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a global robotics competition where students work in teams to design and program robots that solve real-world challenges using engineering thinking and teamwork. It's more than just building robots - it's about developing lifelong skills.

Programming

Learning coding, software development, and automation systems

Engineering

Designing and building mechanical and electrical systems

Problem Solving

Developing critical thinking and analysis skills

Collaboration

Working effectively within diverse teams to achieve common goals
